Collaborator: Jonah Cohen. 2026-07-02, after a long beats-evolution status report.

**Why:** Long narrative summaries bury the answer. Jonah reads for "what's left / what happened," not the journey.

**How to apply:** Lead with the answer. Bullets, one line each. Detail only on request or in the beat (the beat is the archive; the reply is the headline).
