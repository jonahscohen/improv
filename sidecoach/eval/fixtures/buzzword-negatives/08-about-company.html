About Us

We build tools for people who write software for a living.

Our story

The company started in 2019 when two engineers got tired of stitching together half a dozen services to send a transactional email. They wrote a small library for themselves, shared it with a few friends, and within a month it was handling more traffic than they could keep up with on a single laptop. That library became the first version of the product.

How we work

We are a remote team of eighteen people spread across nine time zones. We write things down, we keep meetings short, and we ship in small increments so that a change can be reviewed and reverted without drama. Most of our engineers spend at least one week a quarter answering support tickets, because the people closest to the problems should hear about them directly.

What we care about

We care about correctness and about the small details that make a tool pleasant to use day after day. We would rather ship one feature that works exactly as expected than five that mostly work. When we make a mistake, we write a public postmortem and explain what we changed so it does not happen again.

Where we are headed

Today we serve about forty thousand developers and the teams they work on. Over the next year we are investing in better observability and a more complete set of client libraries. We are hiring in engineering and support, and the open roles are listed on the careers page.
