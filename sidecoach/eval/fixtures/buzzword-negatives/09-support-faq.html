Help Center

Answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the sign-in page and select "Forgot password." Enter the email address on your account and we will send a reset link that is valid for one hour. If the email does not arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder and confirm you entered the address you registered with.

How do I invite a teammate?

Open Settings, then Members, and choose "Invite." Enter the person's email address and pick a role. They will receive an invitation link and can join even if they do not have an account yet. Invitations expire after seven days.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Every plan includes a full export from Settings, Data. Exports are generated as a downloadable archive of JSON files and usually finish within a few minutes. There is no charge and no limit on how often you can export.

How is billing prorated?

When you upgrade mid-cycle we charge the difference for the remaining days at the new rate. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of the next cycle and we do not refund the difference for the current period.

How do I cancel?

Open Settings, Billing, and choose "Cancel plan." Your account remains on the paid plan until the end of the current billing period, after which it reverts to the free plan. Your data is retained and nothing is deleted by cancelling.

How do I contact support?

Paid plans can email support directly and receive a reply within one business day. Free accounts can post in the community forum, where both our team and other users answer questions.
