Advanced, enterprise-grade infrastructure

Modern, robust, and scalable systems for engineering teams that run production workloads.

Built on proven foundations

The platform runs on Kubernetes with a control plane we have operated for six years. Each tenant is isolated at the namespace level, and resource quotas keep one workload from starving another. We publish our architecture and our capacity headroom so you can plan around real numbers.

Observability included

Every service emits structured logs, metrics, and traces by default. You can query the last thirty days from the dashboard, set alerts on any metric, and export to your own collector if you already run one. There is no separate add-on to buy for basic visibility.

Access control

Roles map to the actions a person can take, not to a fixed set of tiers. You can grant a contractor read access to one project for two weeks and have it expire automatically. Every privileged action is recorded in an audit log you can export.

Support that knows the system

Support is staffed by engineers who have operated the platform, not a separate tier reading from a script. On the business plan you get a shared channel and a named contact who escalates directly to the on-call rotation.
