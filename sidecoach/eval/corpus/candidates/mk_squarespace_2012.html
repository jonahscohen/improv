What is it?

A fully hosted, completely managed environment for creating and maintaining a beautiful website, blog, or portfolio.

Who is it for?

Anyone who wants to create an extraordinary website. Bloggers. Businesses. Photographers. Our flexible platform powers sites of all sizes.

Why use it?

We give you full control over your content and site customization. You can build a website or create a blog 10X faster on our comprehensive platform.

Templates That Aren't Templated

85+ professionally designed style variants from brand-name designers, all with point-and-click control over every design element.

Free Custom Domain Names

Every annual and bi-annual account receives a free custom domain, giving you the most complete website service possible.

Social Integration

Social widgets let you aggregate data from across your social networks completely within the Squarespace environment.

Beautiful Photo Galleries

Customizable galleries let you choose how to present your work. Lightbox integration, hover effects, and image resizing are a few perks.

Squarespace for iOS and Android

Our mobile apps are completely hand-built. Post to your blog, manage comments, and see detailed statistics, all while on the go.

Know Your Visitors

Our powerful analytics tools let you view your site traffic in real time. Learn where visitors are coming from, and how they found you.

Seamless Blog Importing

Whatever platform you're coming from, we'll move over your images, copy your posts, and make sure all your links work.

Intuitive Editing

Want to add content? Edit something you already wrote? No problem. Our rapid content-editing tool will help you every step of the way.

Form Building & Data Collection

Our Unlimited plan comes with an industrial-strength form builder, that contains 14 field types, and collects data via email or spreadsheet.
