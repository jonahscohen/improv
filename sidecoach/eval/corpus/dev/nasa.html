Super Puff Planets

Data from NASA’s TESS mission has revealed two new “super‑puff” planets — giant worlds so lightweight that their density is comparable to cotton candy. The Jupiter‑sized planets, TOI‑791 b and TOI‑791 c, are considered the "puffiest" worlds ever discovered.
