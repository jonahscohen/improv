# app-ui-corporate-dense-it-operations (coverage brief, Codex-authored)

title: IT Operations Incident Console
register: app-ui
aestheticStyle: corporate-dense
audience: IT operations teams monitoring incidents across enterprise systems.
goal: Enable operators to triage active incidents and coordinate response actions.
requiredContent:
- Active incident list
- Severity, owner, service, and timestamp information
- Incident detail area
- Status history
- Related alerts
- Assignment and escalation actions
- Search and filtering
- Empty and error states
constraints:
- audience: Users work under time pressure and need reliable operational information.
- brand-tone-in-words: Efficient, authoritative, precise, and controlled.
- platform: Desktop-first web application with responsive considerations.
- baseline: Must be accessible, keyboard-usable, and populated with fictional incident data.
successCriteria:
- Operators can prioritize incidents accurately.
- Ownership, status, and next actions are unambiguous.
- Dense operational information remains manageable during triage.
