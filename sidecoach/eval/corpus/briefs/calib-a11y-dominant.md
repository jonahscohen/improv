# Patient intake form (calibration brief)

- register: forms
- audience: patients of all ages and abilities completing intake at a clinic, often on phones, sometimes with low vision or motor constraints
- goal: let a patient complete a multi-section intake form quickly and without error.
- required content: personal details, insurance, medical history (multi-select), consent checkboxes, a progress indicator, a submit action, inline validation messaging.
- constraints: must work at 320px width; keyboard-operable; honor prefers-reduced-motion; brand color is a mid-saturation teal (#1f8a8a) on white; system font stack.
- success criteria: a first-time user finishes without confusion; every field is reachable and legible; errors are understandable; nothing depends on color or hover alone.
