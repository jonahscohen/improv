# forms-multi-step-corporate-vendor-onboarding (coverage brief, Codex-authored)

title: Vendor Onboarding Application
register: forms
aestheticStyle: multi-step-corporate
audience: Suppliers applying to work with a mid-sized enterprise procurement team.
goal: Guide vendors through submitting company, compliance, and payment details for review.
requiredContent:
- Step overview
- Company profile fields
- Primary contact information
- Compliance attestations
- Tax and payment details
- Document upload placeholders
- Review step
- Submission confirmation
constraints:
- audience: Applicants may need to gather information from several departments.
- brand-tone-in-words: Formal, clear, procedural, and helpful.
- platform: Responsive web form suitable for desktop completion.
- baseline: Must meet common accessibility expectations and support saving or returning as a described capability.
successCriteria:
- Applicants understand progress and remaining requirements.
- Complex information is broken into manageable sections.
- The completed submission can be reviewed before final send.
