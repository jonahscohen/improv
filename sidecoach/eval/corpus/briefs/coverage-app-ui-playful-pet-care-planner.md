# app-ui-playful-pet-care-planner (coverage brief, Codex-authored)

title: Pet Care Planner App
register: app-ui
aestheticStyle: playful
audience: Pet owners coordinating feeding, medication, appointments, and shared care tasks.
goal: Help households manage daily pet care responsibilities without missing important tasks.
requiredContent:
- Pet profile summary
- Today’s care tasks
- Medication reminders
- Upcoming appointments
- Shared caregiver assignments
- Add task flow entry point
- Completion and overdue states
constraints:
- audience: Users may include multiple household members with different levels of commitment.
- brand-tone-in-words: Friendly, reassuring, lively, and responsible.
- platform: Mobile-first app interface with responsive support.
- baseline: Must meet common accessibility expectations and use fictional pet and task data.
successCriteria:
- Care responsibilities are easy to understand and update.
- Urgent items are distinguishable from routine tasks.
- The interface supports shared household coordination.
