# Independent magazine essay hero (calibration brief)

- register: editorial
- audience: readers arriving at a long-form essay from social links, expecting a considered editorial voice.
- goal: set the tone for a 4,000-word essay so a reader wants to begin reading.
- required content: a title, a standfirst/deck, byline + date, an opening pull element, and the first paragraphs of body text.
- constraints: a single serif display face + one text face; a restrained two-color palette on an off-white ground; no stock imagery; content-first (no decorative chrome).
- success criteria: the hierarchy and rhythm feel intentional; the type pairing and spacing read as crafted; the reader's eye is led from title into body without friction.
