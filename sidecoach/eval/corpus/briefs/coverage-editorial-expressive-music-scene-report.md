- Article title
- Standfirst

- audience: Readers expect personality, specificity, and cultural context.
- brand-tone-in-words: Vivid, curious, contemporary, and credible.
- platform: Responsive digital editorial feature.
- baseline: Must meet common accessibility expectations and use fictional people, venues, and quotes.

- The piece conveys why the scene matters now.
- Artist, venue, and community perspectives are easy to distinguish.
- The article feels engaging without sacrificing clarity.
