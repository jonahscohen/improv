# marketing-landing-minimal-language-app (coverage brief, Codex-authored)

title: Language Practice Subscription
register: marketing/landing
aestheticStyle: minimal
audience: Busy adults who want a manageable daily language-learning habit.
goal: Introduce a subscription language practice app and encourage trial sign-ups.
requiredContent:
- Product name: DailyPhrase
- One-sentence product promise
- Main benefits
- Sample daily lesson content described in words
- Subscription options
- Testimonials from three user types
- Trial sign-up action
constraints:
- audience: Users have limited time and may compare against other learning tools.
- brand-tone-in-words: Calm, clear, encouraging, and efficient.
- platform: Responsive web landing page.
- baseline: Must be accessible, readable, and self-contained with placeholder text only.
successCriteria:
- The offering is understandable within a short visit.
- The trial path is clear without pressuring the user.
- The page communicates habit-building value and trustworthiness.
