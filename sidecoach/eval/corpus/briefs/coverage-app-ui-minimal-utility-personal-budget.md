# app-ui-minimal-utility-personal-budget (coverage brief, Codex-authored)

title: Personal Budget Dashboard
register: app-ui
aestheticStyle: minimal-utility
audience: Adults tracking monthly household spending and savings goals.
goal: Help users review their finances and make routine budget adjustments.
requiredContent:
- Current month summary
- Income, spending, and savings goal status
- Category spending list
- Recent transactions
- Alerts for unusual activity
- Budget adjustment controls
- Empty state for new users
constraints:
- audience: Users need quick comprehension and confidence when handling money.
- brand-tone-in-words: Clear, practical, calm, and supportive.
- platform: Responsive web app interface.
- baseline: Must meet common accessibility expectations and use fictional financial data.
successCriteria:
- Users can identify budget status at a glance.
- Common review and adjustment tasks are straightforward.
- Sensitive information is presented with appropriate clarity and discretion.
