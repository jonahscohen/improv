# forms-minimal-medical-intake (coverage brief, Codex-authored)

title: New Patient Intake Form
register: forms
aestheticStyle: minimal
audience: Adults completing intake information before a primary care appointment.
goal: Collect necessary patient information accurately while reducing confusion and friction.
requiredContent:
- Patient contact details
- Insurance information
- Emergency contact
- Current medications
- Allergies
- Medical history checklist
- Consent acknowledgement
- Review and submit action
constraints:
- audience: Users may be stressed, time-limited, or using assistive technology.
- brand-tone-in-words: Respectful, clear, calm, and professional.
- platform: Responsive web form.
- baseline: Must meet common accessibility expectations and clearly communicate required versus optional fields.
successCriteria:
- Users can complete the form with confidence.
- Sensitive information requests feel appropriate and understandable.
- Validation and review needs are clear before submission.
