CockroachDB is a cloud-agnostic, PostgreSQL compatible database built for modern applications that is the inevitable choice delivering unmatched uptime, scale and compliance - anywhere in the world. 

Trusted by industry leaders to solve their toughest data problems and build what’s next.

From AI to fintech, CockroachDB powers business critical systems

One platform. Four core advantages.

Deploy and run anywhere across on-premise, hybrid, and cloud. On our cloud or yours.

Enable state-of-the-art resilience.

Survive regional failures with zero data loss, automated failover, and strong consistency. No runbooks and minimal maintenance windows. Online upgrades, schema changes, and backups keep everything running smoothly.

Industry leading fintechs and banks rely on CockroachDB for continuous uptime across 50M+ users.

Scale seamlessly.

Eliminate manual sharding and scale effortlessly with consistent performance. Start small, scale to hundreds of nodes and petabytes—no app re-architecture required.

CockroachDB enables CoreWeave to scale to trillions of objects while maintaining strong consistency and maintaining always-on availability.

Deploy global and compliant applications.

Implement declarative policies to pin data in specific localities and geographic locations. Launch into global markets by enabling compliance and keeping data close to users to reduce latency. 


SumUp, a global fintech for 4M+ merchants across 35 markets, relies on CockroachDB to adhere to regulations such as Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) without per country databases.

Streamline operations and drive cost efficiencies at scale.

Achieve high cost efficiency and consolidate databases on a modern PostgreSQL platform. Simplify migrations with MOLT and stream real-time updates to analytics across any environment, from laptops to Kubernetes.


By moving from traditional PostgreSQL to CockroachDB, Riskified was able to see 83% lower query latency, 4.5x higher throughput and scale on demand all without requiring massive changes to their application code.
